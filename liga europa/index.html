Fenerbahce

THE Fenerbahçe set up in a 3-4-3 formation. Altai was under the goalposts. The defensive trio consisted of Kim, Salai and Novak. The midfield quartet consisted of Osagi, Hakan, Gustavo and Kantioglu, while the attacking quartet consisted of Rossi, Pelkas and Valencia. Olympiakos played against the given order like UEFA with four in defense. With Vatslik under the goal post, right back Ba, left back Reabciuk and Papastathopoulos – Cisse in the center of the defense. Bouchalakis was a cutter, ahead of internal midfielders Mandi and Agibu Kamara. On the right wing were Masouras, Oniekourou on the left and Ticinio at the head.
